Therapeutic Techniques That Support Your Healing

Functional Fitness / Corrective Movement

Targeted exercises to correct muscle imbalances, improve movement patterns, and prevent future injuries. Our doctors create personalized programs based on your specific needs.

Massage Therapy

Therapeutic massage to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and enhance the effects of chiropractic adjustments. Various techniques available based on your condition.

Therapeutic Ultrasound

Using sound waves to promote tissue healing, reduce inflammation, and alleviate pain. This non-invasive therapy is especially effective for soft tissue injuries.

Electrical Stimulation

Gentle electrical currents applied to targeted areas to reduce pain, decrease muscle spasms, and accelerate the healing process.
